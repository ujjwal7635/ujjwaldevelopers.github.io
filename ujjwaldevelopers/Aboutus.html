Headquarter is located at Jalgaon district
Ujjwal Developers Limited was incorporated by Vivek Chaudhari in 2001. The group has worked with contractors such as Aedas , and two of its joint-development projects were in collaboration with UA Group. It had also collaborated with Dubai-based Dugdug Construction. In 2008, Ujjwal Developers planned to build a Special Enterprise Zone (SEZ) in Jalgaon at an estimated cost of $660M US. The development was to be on a 255-acre (103 ha) site but the project did not advance. The Government of India announced denotification of the SEZ project on 29 January 2014; the entire project was scrapped.
